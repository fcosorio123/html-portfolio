The Best Books According to Favio

My top 3 books of all-time.

Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir

This is a thrilling science fiction novel about an astronaut who wakes up with no memory aboard a spaceship, the Hail Mary, on a desperate mission to save Earth from an extinction-level threat. Through ingenuity, science, and unexpected camaraderie with an alien, he battles to remember his mission and save both his world and an alien species

How to Be a Stock Market Genius by Joel Greenblatt

This book is an accessible guide to finding undervalued stocks and market inefficiencies. Greenblatt shares strategies for individual investors to identify high-return investments through special situations like spin-offs, mergers, and restructurings, stressing the importance of thorough research and a disciplined approach to investing.
